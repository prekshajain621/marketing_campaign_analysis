# marketing_campaign_analysis
## Description
The analysis is provided for designing and development of marketing campaign Performance dashboard using Excel, SQL and PowerBI. The purpose of creating this dashboard is to gain insightful insights to maximize the profit for campaign Performance.
Tools Used:- Excel, SQL, Power BI 
📁 Dataset:- More than 2000 Customer profile from maven analytics.

**DATASET USED:- **
The dataset contains customer-level and marketing campaign data including-
1. Impressions, Clicks, Conversions, Cost 
2. Products (Wines, Fruits, Meat, Fish, Sweets, Gold)
3. Campaign Responses(AcceptedCmp1 to AcceptedCmp5)

## 📈 Power BI Dashboard - Marketing Campaign Insights 
After solving the questions for improvemnet in campaign. I build an interactive Dashboard in Power BI. 

📌 **Key features**
**1. Filters on the data**
Revenue Comparison, ROI comparison, conversion comparison and funnel chart 

📊 **Key visuals and insights**
- TOTAL CUSTOMERS:- Total Customers represents the total number of unique customers in the dataset.
- TOTAL IMPRESSIONS: -  Total Impressions indicates the number of times marketing content was displayed/seen.
- TOTAL CLICKS: - Total Clicks shows user engagement or how many times a user has clicked an ad.
- CTR (Click Through Rate):-  CTR is the percentage of people who click on specific link, ad, or email after seeing it.
- CPC (Cost Per Click):-  Cost Per Click is a digital ad metric that measures how much an advertiser pays each time someone clicks on their online ad.
- CONVERSION RATE:- measures the percentage of users who completed the desired action such as making a purchase or clicking a link.
- TOTAL REVENUE:- Total Revenue is the total amount of money a business earns from selling goods or services during a specific period.
- ROI (Return on Investment):-  ROI measures the profitability of the marketing campaigns.

🔍 **Campaign Insights**

2. Wines contribute to the highest revenue product. 
3. People prefer to purchase from Store channel.
4. Low CTR indicates weak ad engagement. 
5. Funnel analysis shows major drop off at click stage.
